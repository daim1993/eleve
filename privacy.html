Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-01

Elevé ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What we collect

We use privacy-first, cookie-free analytics: an anonymous page-view event (the page path and time) is recorded only after you accept the banner. We do not set advertising cookies, do not use third-party trackers, and do not collect names, emails, IP-based profiles, or any personal identifiers through analytics.

Accounts & the room builder

If you create an account, we store your email and a securely hashed password. Projects you save to the cloud are stored to provide the service. You can delete projects at any time.

The content editor (CMS)

Edits made in the CMS are stored in your browser and, when published, on our server to display the site.

Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by contacting creative@sentientbyelysian.com. Where GDPR/UK-GDPR applies, our lawful bases are consent (analytics) and contract (accounts).

Data retention & security

We keep account and project data until you delete it or close your account. Passwords are salted and hashed; transport should be secured with HTTPS in production.
